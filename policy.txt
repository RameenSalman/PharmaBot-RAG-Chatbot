MediCare Pharmacy - Store Policies

Shipping Policy
- Standard delivery: 2-4 business days.
- Express delivery: 1 business day in eligible cities.
- Orders over Rs. 2500 qualify for free standard shipping.

Prescription Policy
- Prescription-only medicines require a valid prescription before dispatch.
- Prescriptions are reviewed by a licensed pharmacist.

Returns & Refunds
- Medicines cannot be returned after delivery unless they are damaged, expired, or supplied incorrectly.
- Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after approval.

Payment Methods
- Visa, Mastercard, debit cards, bank transfer, and cash on delivery (selected areas).

Privacy Policy
- Customer information is used only for order processing and support.
- Personal data is not shared with third parties except delivery partners when necessary.

Customer Support
- Support hours: Monday-Friday, 9:00 AM-6:00 PM.
- Email responses are typically provided within 24 hours.

Order Tracking
- Customers receive a tracking link by email after shipment.
- Tracking is also available in the My Orders section.

Storage Guidance
- Store medicines according to the instructions provided on their packaging.
- Keep medicines out of reach of children.
